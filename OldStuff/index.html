About Us

The UW Red Cross Association is about fulfilling the Canadian Red Cross mission: to better the lives of susceptible people everywhere through people like you and I. By hosting fundraising and promotional activities, we hope to raise awareness of the needs around us. Let us, the leaders of tomorrow, change the world one step at a time.

"Be the change you wish to see in the world." - Mahatma Gandhi
